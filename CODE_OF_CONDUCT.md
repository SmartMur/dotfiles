# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and professional
- Accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on the long-term health of the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and public spaces when
someone is representing this project.

## Reporting

For behavior concerns, contact maintainers through project support channels.
For security concerns, follow `SECURITY.md`.
